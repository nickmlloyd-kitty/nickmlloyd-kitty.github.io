Somewhere deep in the English countryside, Carlton Lodge is holding its breath. There’s a wedding to plan, a scheming MP to outwit, and a houseful of eccentric relations to keep from open warfare.

Through it all, a stuffed grizzly bear keeps dignified watch over the chaos.

Warm, witty and brimming with heart, Wholly Matrimony opens the Carlton Lodge Trilogy — the perfect curl-up-and-disappear read.
